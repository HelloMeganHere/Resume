About Me

Hello! My name is Megan. I'm learning how to create websites!

It's been a long time since I learned HTML. I first started my HTML journey on Neopets, but to pivoted over to photoshop and 3D programs. I went to school for digital animation, and worked on several projects and even had an internship at DreamWorks' Big Idea, where I worked on Netflix's Veggietales in the house!. (Here is my demo reel if you are interested)

After about a year of freelancing in the animation industry, I found an opportunity through a coworker who introduced me to his wife. She got me a job doing maintenance, then I pivoted over to data entry. From there I was promoted to an analyst doing inventory management. I ended up working at that company for 8 years. I learned a lot about supply chain, and more importantly myself.

While I was there, I learned about computer languages, specifically, SQL. This opened a flood gate for me, and I became incredily passionate about learning SQL. I still missed my creative side, and I was trying to figure out how I could marry by Supply Chain side with creativity. I learned about PowerBI! This is exactly what I have been looking for.

I've spent the last year learning PowerBI and the last several months learning Python, HTML, CSS and other development practices. My goal is to one day become a Data Analyst or Business Analyst. Or even a web developmer. Or all three!
